Work at JavaJam

 

Barista ($15 hourly + tips)

As a barista at JavaJam, you will take orders at the front and prepare specality beverages
for customers. Baristas will be expected to work in a very fast-paced environment, as well as work with other
team members to maintain the face of the storefront. You will also be expected to..

  • Maintain cleanliness of stations
  • Lift up to 20lbs to help restock items

 

Maintence Crew ($23 hourly)

As a member of the maintence crew, you will help deal with daily maintence to the store, like cleaning, preparing
stage for music nights, as well as redecorating. You may also be expected to fix issues like leaks or repairs.
This roll is very labor intensive and works with several chemcicals used in cleaning.

  • Expected to lift very heavy objects (30lbs-60lbs)
  • Maintain cleanliness of whole store
  • Help preformers preare for stage shows by setting up stage.
  • Demonstate knowledge of repairing certain appliances
